How to choose the right soap.

There are a variety of things to consider when buying our soap. We have two types of soap: Goat’s Milk & Farmer’s Soap.

Goat’s Milk is a high lather soap, usually the colors are also more vibrant. Farmer’s Soap cleans well, and is a long lasting soap.
